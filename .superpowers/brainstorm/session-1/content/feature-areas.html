What areas interest you most?

Gruuber already has: Auth · Ride Booking · Food Orders · Real-time Tracking · Payments · CQRS/Kafka. Select any feature areas you'd like to explore adding.

A

🗓 Scheduled Rides & Orders

Let users pre-book rides or food delivery for a future time. Requires a scheduling engine and reminder notifications.

B

🚗 Ride Pooling / Carpooling

Match multiple riders going the same direction. Reduces cost for riders, increases driver utilization. Adds route optimization complexity.

C

⭐ Ratings & Reviews

Bidirectional rating system — riders rate drivers, drivers rate riders, users rate restaurants. Feeds into the driver matching score already defined.

D

💬 In-App Chat

Real-time messaging between rider and driver (or customer and restaurant). Built on SignalR, fits naturally into the existing architecture.

E

⚡ Surge Pricing

Dynamic price multipliers based on demand vs driver supply in a region. Already listed as "Could Have" in the spec — ready to design.

F

🎟 Promotions & Discount Codes

Coupon/promo system for riders and restaurants. Also spec'd as "Could Have". Connects to the payment flow for discount application.

G

🎫 Subscription / Gruuber Pass

Monthly subscription that gives riders discounted rides or free delivery. Recurring billing, entitlement checks at order/ride creation.

H

🆘 Safety Features (SOS)

In-ride SOS button, trip sharing with a contact, driver identity verification photo. Critical for user trust in a ride-hailing platform.

I

📊 Driver & Restaurant Dashboards

Earnings summaries, performance metrics, and order history for drivers and restaurant owners. Read-model driven, low operational cost.

J

📍 Multi-Stop Rides

Riders add intermediate stops in a single trip. Fare calculated per segment. Extends the ride state machine with waypoint transitions.
